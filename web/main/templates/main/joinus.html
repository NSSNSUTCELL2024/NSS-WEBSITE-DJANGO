Join US - for creating a change in the society

The NSS NSUT Cell welcomes enthusiastic and service-minded students to become part of our team. We are dedicated to contributing to society while building leadership and teamwork skills. Below is a detailed explanation of the recruitment process for becoming an NSS volunteer at our university.

1. Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for NSS, you must be an enrolled student of NSUT, preferably from the first or second year. A strong spirit of service and social responsibility is required.

2. Application Process

Keep an eye on the recruitment notifications that are announced at the start of the academic year. Interested students must fill out the provided application form, which usually includes personal details and a statement of purpose.

3. Selection Process

Shortlisted applicants may undergo an interview or group discussion to assess their commitment and interest in social work.

4. Volunteer Requirements

Selected volunteers are expected to complete 120 hours of service per academic year, participate in various events like blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, and educational initiatives.

5. Training & Orientation

All selected volunteers undergo an orientation to NSS philosophy, skill-building workshops, and awareness sessions on social issues.

6. Assessment & Rewards

Volunteers are regularly assessed based on participation, and upon completion of their service, they receive certificates of merit. Exceptional volunteers are recognized through awards.

7. Benefits of Joining NSS

NSS provides numerous benefits, including skill development, networking opportunities, career advancement, and personal growth.
